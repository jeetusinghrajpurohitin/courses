Admission Information

📅 Admission Schedule

Admissions open for 2026–27 session. Application windows are available online via the official website or by calling the admissions helpline at 9667048555.

📋 Eligibility

10+2 with minimum 50% aggregate (General) / 45% (Gen-PH) / 40% (Reserved) for UG. Graduate degree with 55% for PG programs. Dental programs require NEET qualification.

📝 Entrance Tests Accepted

KUAT (Karnavati University Admission Test), CUET, JEE Main, GUJCET, CAT, CMAT, CLAT, LNAT-UK, and NEET UG/PG depending on the program.

🏆 Scholarships

Merit-based scholarships and financial aid options available. Contact the scholarship cell for details. The university also offers discounts and installment payment options.
